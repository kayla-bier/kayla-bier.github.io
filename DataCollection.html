Data Collection

Oberserved using the McDonald Observatory in Fort Davis, TX
Observed 6 face-on spiral galaxies:
  • M51
  • M61
  • M99
  • M100
  • NGC 3344
  • NGC 3938
Observed in three different filters: B,V,R
Exposure times ranged from 60-120 seconds
Binning: 1x1
Three images were taken of each galaxy in each filter
All observations were perfomed April 12, 2023
